Indian Cricket Captain

Men's Test Cricket Captains

  • C. K. Nayudu (First Test Captain)
  • Mansoor Ali Khan Pataudi (Tiger Pataudi) (Led in 40 Tests)
  • Sunil Gavaskar (One of the greatest openers and captains)
  • Kapil Dev (1983 World Cup winning captain)
  • Mohammad Azharuddin (Longest serving captain in 90s)
  • Sachin Tendulkar (Brief stints, legendary batsman)
  • Sourav Ganguly (Transformed Indian cricket)
  • Rahul Dravid (The Wall, brief captaincy)
  • MS Dhoni (Most successful Indian captain across formats)
  • Virat Kohli (Most successful Test captain in terms of wins)
  • Rohit Sharma (Recent Test captain, also leads ODIs)
  • Shubman Gill (Current Test captain as of May 2025)

Men's ODI Cricket Captains (Key)

  • Ajit Wadekar (First ODI Captain)
  • Kapil Dev (1983 World Cup Winner)
  • Mohammad Azharuddin (Longest ODI captaincy stint in 90s)
  • Sourav Ganguly (Led India to 2003 World Cup Final)
  • MS Dhoni (2011 World Cup & 2013 Champions Trophy Winner)
  • Virat Kohli (High winning percentage)
  • Rohit Sharma (Current ODI captain)

Men's T20I Cricket Captains (Key)

  • Virender Sehwag (First T20I Captain)
  • MS Dhoni (2007 T20 World Cup Winner)
  • Rohit Sharma (Most wins as T20I captain, 2024 T20 World Cup Winner)
  • Suryakumar Yadav (Current T20I captain)

Women's Cricket Captains (Key)

  • Shantha Rangaswamy (First Test Captain)
  • Diana Edulji (Early pioneer of Indian Women's Cricket)
  • Mithali Raj (Longest-serving captain, prolific run-scorer)
  • Harmanpreet Kaur (Current captain across formats, T20 specialist)

This is a simplified list focusing on key captains. Many other great players have also had the honor of leading the Indian cricket team for various matches or series.
